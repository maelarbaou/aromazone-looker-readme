Core Ambition

Create one shared platform to steer purchasing performance, from executive oversight to detailed operational analysis by SKU, reference, supplier, category or buyer.

Business Scope

Track purchasing spend and PPV performance by comparing budget, actuals to date and future projections, highlighting gaps between forecast and execution.

Drill-Down Model

Enable progressive navigation across levels: management view, business poles such as Packaging, Raw Materials and Subcontracting, then dynamic detailed filters.

Consolidate current Looker dashboards and Excel-based processes into one automated, reliable and stabilized source of truth.
